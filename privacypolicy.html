Privacy Policy

Stance does not store any user data. The user's responses to the survey are stored locally to generate scores, and never stored in the long term. We use local storage— that is, storage that is stored on your own computer, not our servers— to use your responses to generate codes.

Privacy matters. Knowledge over you is power over you, and this just doesn't sit right with us. If you wan't to see how the code works, Stance is entirely open-source, available on Github.
